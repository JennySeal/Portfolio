About

When Jenny Seal was a girl, she enjoyed many happy hours coding on her ZX Spectrum. In her early 20s she became an Analyst Programmer, coding in MAPPER, for the IT consultancy firm, Cap Gemini in Glasgow.

Then Jenny moved to London and switched direction working for a Christian charity in communications and events. In 2009 she moved to Norwich, and for the next ten years worked in project management for a charity, wrote web copy and news articles for a website and newspaper, while raising her two daughters.

Two weeks before Lockdown 1, Jenny, her family and Miss Moneypenny the cat, moved to Cardiff. Jenny has spent the last year becoming fluent in HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Python alongside home schooling, decorating and training their pandemic pup, Toffy.

Jenny is now ready to create beautiful, interactive, responsive and accessible websites.
